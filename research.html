Research & Student Opportunities

The Shore Innovation Center promotes interdisciplinary research by connecting students, faculty, and industry partners. Participants can engage in hands-on projects, explore emerging technologies, and gain valuable real-world experience.

Faculty Research Areas

Faculty members collaborate across engineering, computing, and science disciplines to address complex challenges in cybersecurity, advanced manufacturing, artificial intelligence, and robotics.

Student Opportunities

  • Undergraduate research assistant positions
  • Faculty-led innovation projects
  • Industry-sponsored collaborations
  • Internships and experiential learning
  • Participation in engineering and computing competitions
